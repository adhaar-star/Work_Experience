FAQs

What is Gist?

Gist is a new medium to express yourself, demonstrate your skillsets and show-off your creativity. On Gist you can create a short, no-pressure video response to a thought provoking question. The world's leading companies are discovering and hiring people on Gist every day.

Why is Gist better than a resume?

Resumes are boring. We just fell asleep thinking about reading one. Do you. And don’t do a resume. Have a Gist instead.

How do I get started?

Gist is available for iOS and you can download it free on the Apple App Store. Signing up takes less than five minutes.

I'm not currently looking for a job, is Gist for me?

Most of the people on Gist are not actively looking for a job. We like to think of Gist as being like that box of pizza rolls buried in your freezer. You may not eat them today, but there will come a time late at night when that frozen dough will be salvation.

We’re hiring, how can we use Gist?

We are super picky about the companies we work with. To learn more about hiring through the platform, click here: www.gistapp.com/hiring

What about my privacy?

Privacy is extremely important to us here at Jerry, so your information is automatically hidden from your current or most recent employer, and is never visible to anyone off platform.

I have more questions, who can I contact?

Talking is good. Get in touch anytime by emailing support@trygist.com
