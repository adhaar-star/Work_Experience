FAQ

  • What is Gist?

    Gist is the fastest way to get interviews and job offers from leading companies and startups. Think of a Gist like you would an Instagram story, but for an audience of stellar companies instead of your followers.

  • How does it work?

    We’ve compiled a list of dynamic questions from industry experts. Pick one you like and record a thirty second video reply. That’s your Gist. We'll then beam it to 500+ top employers because getting job offers is easy when the companies know who you are.

  • How is Gist different?

    Resumes are boring relics of the past. We just fell asleep thinking about reading one. Have a Gist instead.

  • How long does it take?

    It takes less than ten minutes to Join for Gist.

  • How do I get started?

    It's easy. Click here: /candidate/signup

  • Does it matter if my Gist video is vertical or landscape?

    Yes! Your video should be vertical!

  • How long should my Gist be?

    Your Gist must be 30 seconds or less. Anything past 30 seconds will be cut automatically.

  • Any tips for making an effective Gist?

    Go with what's comfortable to you. For a simple video use your phone's standard recorder. If you want to get creative, apps like Instagram and Clips offer more expressive features.

  • Should I wing it or practice?

    Your first impulse may be to get in front of the camera and start talking, but it’s usually better to practice a few times first. But don't get too practiced. Authentic content is honest and imperfect. Imperfect feels real. And employers trust real and reward it with engagement and interview requests.

  • When will employers be able to see my profile?

    Your Gist will be available to employers as soon as you've completed registration.

  • Can I share my Gist?

    You can include your Gist URL on your, ah um, standard resume to liven it up.

  • I'm not currently looking for a job, is Gist for me?

    Most of the people on Gist are not actively looking for a job. We like to think of Gist as being like that box of pizza rolls buried in your freezer. You may not eat them today, but there will come a time late at night when that frozen dough will be your salvation.

  • We’re hiring, how can we use Gist?

    We are super picky about the companies we work with. To learn more about hiring through the Gist platform, click here: /employer/register

  • Is my college a good fit for Gist's affiliate program?

    Yes! We're passionate about making Gist available to college students and alumni. Getting setup is easy, and requires no contracts. Contact our staff for more information — questions@yourgist.com

  • I have a question, who can I contact?

    Email the Gist team at questions@yourgist.com
